### The 5 Questions Before AI Executes a DeFi Action

| Check | Question |
|-------|----------|
| 1. Token | Is the contract address correct? Decimals? Transfer tax? |
| 2. Liquidity | Is there enough depth for this size? What's the price impact? |
| 3. Slippage | Is the slippage bound reasonable for current volatility? |
| 4. Oracle | Is the price feed fresh? Could it be manipulated? |
| 5. Simulation | Does this tx succeed in simulation? Any revert? |

### AI × DeFi: Where to Start vs What to Avoid

**Safe starting points for AI agents:**
- Read-only: portfolio summary, position monitoring, liquidation alerts
- Advisory: strategy drafting, yield comparison, risk scoring
- Assisted: prepare transactions for human signing

**Never delegate without escalation:**
- Raw swap / lend / borrow / leverage
- Token approval (approve/transferFrom)
- Cross-chain bridging
- Collateral withdrawal
